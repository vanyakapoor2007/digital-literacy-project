## Project Overview

This project is part of the Digital Literacy course at VIT Bhopal University. The aim of this project is to understand how to use digital tools effectively and responsibly in academic and professional settings.

Through this project, I explored different aspects of digital literacy such as creating an online presence, communicating professionally, using coding and collaboration platforms, and understanding cyber safety. Each task helped me learn something practical that I can continue to use in the future.

---

## Task Summaries

### Task 1 – Digital Literacy Infographic
In this task, I created an infographic using Canva to explain digital literacy and its importance. I included topics like safe internet practices and useful digital tools for students. This task helped me understand how to present information in a simple and visual way.

---

### Task 2 – Student Digital Portfolio
For this task, I created and updated my profiles on platforms like GitHub and LinkedIn. I learned how to maintain a basic professional presence online and understood why these platforms are important for future opportunities.

---

### Task 3 – Coding & Collaboration Platforms
I completed a beginner-level coding problem on HackerRank to get familiar with coding platforms. I also created a Google Form quiz related to digital literacy and linked it to a response sheet. This helped me understand how online tools can be used for learning and collaboration.

---

### Task 4 – Email Etiquette & Communication
In this task, I wrote two professional emails—one to a professor and one to an internship coordinator. I also created a checklist of do’s and don’ts for social media use. This task helped me understand how important clear and respectful communication is in a professional environment.

---

### Task 5 – Cybercrime Awareness
For this task, I worked on a case study related to cybercrime and created a prevention checklist. I learned about common online threats and how they can be avoided. This made me more aware of how to stay safe online.
