# Task 5 – Cybercrime Awareness

In this task, I researched the topic of cybercrime with a focus on UPI and online payment frauds.

I prepared a case study on how these frauds are generally committed, on whom these frauds are generally committed, and the possible consequences of these frauds. This also helped me understand how easily anyone can fall prey while using online payment systems.

I also prepared a prevention checklist for the students on how they can be safe from these kinds of frauds.

All the related files for the case study and prevention checklist are attached in this folder.
