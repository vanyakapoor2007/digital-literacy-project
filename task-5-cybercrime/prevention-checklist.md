# Stay Safe Online – UPI & Cyber Safety Tips  

- Do not accept unknown UPI collect requests  
- Do not disclose UPI PIN, OTP, and Bank-related information to anyone  
- Do not click on any suspicious link/messages  
- Do not scan unknown sources of QR codes  
- Do not trust unknown callers claiming to be from banks  
- Use authentic apps and only secure internet connections  
- Enable app locks and two-factor authentications  
- Keep checking your bank statements  

## Reporting Cybercrime  

- National Cyber Crime Portal: https://cybercrime.gov.in  
- Helpline Number: 1930
