## Do’s and Don’ts for responsiple social media usage

## Do’s
- Use respectful and professional language.  
- Think before posting content.
- Keep your profiles clean and updated.  
- Verify before sharing facts and other information.
- Keep privacy settings in order.

## Don’ts
- Do not share personal or sensitive information. 
- Do not share offensive and inappropriate content.  
- Do not get into arguments and start to ‘troll’.  
- Do not spread fake and unverified news.  
- Do not misuse someone else’s content and identity.
